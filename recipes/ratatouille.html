Ratatouille Recipe

Description

A traditional French dish that celebrates the fusion of zucchini, eggplant, bell pepper, tomato into a harmonious spiral pleasing to the palate. A healthy dish that is incredibly rich and satisfying. It pairs marvelously with a slice of French bread, simply iconic!

Ingredients

  • 1 small eggplant
  • 2 small zucchini, sliced
  • 3 Roma tomatoes, sliced
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 small onion, chopped
  • 1/3 cup of carrots, shredded
  • 14 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 F
  2. In a large nonstick pan, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium high heat
  3. Add in onion, garlic, and carrot and cook until tender (~5 minutes)
  4. Add in the crushed tomatoes and seasonings and simmer for 15 minutes
  5. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper
  6. Once the sauce has thickened, pour into a 2 quart baking dish
  7. Arrange sliced vegetables in a circular pattern standing upright in the dish over the sauce
  8. Brush with the remaining olive oil
  9. Cover and bake for 30 minutes, then uncover and bake until vegetables are tender
  10. Serve immediately
